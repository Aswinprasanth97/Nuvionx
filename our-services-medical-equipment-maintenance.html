Maximize Equipment Lifespan, Minimize Failures, Ensure Patient Safety

Our expert clinical engineers provide comprehensive maintenance for a wide range of medical equipment, from infusion pumps and patient monitors to anaesthetic and life-support systems. Regular servicing ensures compliance with MHRA and OEM standards, reduces downtime, and improves patient safety while delivering measurable cost savings.

Expert care for your medical equipment—anytime, anywhere

At Nuvionx, maintenance and repair form the foundation of our service excellence. Our skilled clinical engineers are trained across an extensive range of medical devices and maintain current certifications through ongoing OEM training and manufacturer protocols. This commitment ensures we deliver high-quality service aligned with the latest safety and performance standards.

Whether you manage a few critical assets or thousands of devices across multiple departments, we offer flexible, tailored support solutions. Our comprehensive service portfolio includes reactive repairs, scheduled preventive maintenance, calibration services, and fully managed maintenance contracts designed to meet your specific operational needs
