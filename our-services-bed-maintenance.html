Keeping hospital beds safe, functional, and compliant

We offer specialized maintenance and repair services for a wide range of hospital beds, including electric, ICU, maternity, and bariatric models. Our comprehensive approach ensures that every bed is functioning optimally and safely. Services include detailed mechanical inspections, motor and actuator testing, control panel diagnostics, height and tilt calibration, and safety feature verification. We also focus on hygiene-critical components to support infection control protocols. By conducting regular preventive maintenance, we help healthcare providers minimize bed downtime, enhance patient comfort, and maintain a high standard of care across all clinical settings. With Nuvionx, your beds remain reliable, compliant, and ready for use when they’re needed most.
